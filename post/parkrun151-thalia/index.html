Parkrun 151 - Thalia

January 23, 2017

Good morning and welcome to parkrun 151, which also happens to be the total number of Pok√©mon in the original set.

It seems to be a bit cold this morning so let’s warm up with a bit of clapping.

Rub hands, ok, ready?

Let’s hear it for our volunteers Aimee on stopwatch, Jasmin & Andrew on finish tokens, Julie & Rebekah behind the scanner, Jonathan bringing up the rear and Lucy behind the camera.

If you too would like a standing ovation all you have to do is sign up to be a volunteer.

Getting warmer?

How about a clap for the first timers? Any first timers here today? Yay!

What abour visitors? Where are you from? Let’s hear it for ….

More clapping for everyone who brought their barcodes. Yay! Remember parkrun’s rule: “no barcode, no time”.

Ok, keep the claps going for this week’s anniversary girl. Thalia!

Thalia and I have a lot in common. We both like running. We both like food. We both run so we can eat more food. What’s your favourite dessert Thalia? I like all of them. Other loves of Thalia’s include spending time with family at parkrun, chatting with friends during parkrun, jumping for the camera at parkrun and netball. She’s also done many other longer events such as Semaphore Bravehearts, Mothers Day Classic, Meningie Australia day fun run and the Kuitpo trail run. Even more reasons for good food. Her PB is just shy of 25 minutes and if she chats a bit less while running she’ll go even faster. Congratulations Thalia!

You may have seen on our FB page that we are trying to raise $2000 to buy a defibrilator for our parkrun. We hope we’ll never have to use it, but I have heard quite a few parkrun stories which have had a happy ending because of a defibrilator. Please visit the page a donate what you can.

In other announcements MtB Little Athletics is having a Family Fun day on 5 Feb at 1100 at MTB High School. Check their FB page for details.

That’s it from me! Over to you Aimee.
